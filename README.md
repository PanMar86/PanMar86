## Hello there 👋

My name is Panagiotis Marentakis and I am a certified medical physicist (RPE, MPE) based in Greece.

I use programming to automate daily clinical tasks, while expressing my creative side. I love taking abstract and vague concepts and turn them into compact software solutions that improve the quality of the healthcare services.
I am mostly interested in medical image processing and radiation oncology.

As a person in general, I am a strong believer in continuous learning and I constantly seek new challenges and opportunities.
